About Us

What we do:

The Deepwood Strings has been making beautiful music for distinctive public and private events in the Willamette Valley, Portland and the Oregon Coast areas for over twenty years. Named for Salem's historic Deepwood Estate, we've since performed for weddings, receptions, church events, parties, holiday get-togethers, family reunions, fund raisers, auctions, festivals, banquets, memorial services and other milestone events.

Repertoire:

Our large repertoire includes Baroque, Classical, Romantic, popular, theme and Celtic music. We can tailor a program just for you. Our clients and their guests agree, that live string music adds elegance to their special occasion.

Tailored to Your Event:

The Deepwood Strings can vary in size from two to four players: string quartet, trio or duo. Prices vary depending on your distance from Salem, length of performance and size of the group. The quartet has the largest repertoire and variety, and greatest volume.
